The Rebuild.

On waking up from autopilot, the patterns underneath, and the rebuild that followed.

For years I ran at full speed on the only script I knew. From the outside everything tracked, but inside, something was off. On January 20, 2025, I stopped, pressed a hard reset, and rebuilt from the ground up in silence. A year later, I’d reached a baseline, physical and mental, I did not think was possible.
